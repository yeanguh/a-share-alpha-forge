# Information Sources

Use this source map to broaden daily collection before scoring.

## Official And Primary Sources

- Macro: National Bureau of Statistics, PBOC, SAFE, NDRC, MOF, MIIT, MOFCOM,
  Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, National Energy
  Administration.
- PBOC open market operations: before daily news collection, check the
  People's Bank of China open market operations page for the report-date
  operation notice:
  `https://www.pbc.gov.cn/zhengcehuobisi/125207/125213/125431/index.html`.
  Record reverse repo/MLF/OMO operation amount, maturity, rate, net injection
  or withdrawal, and whether the current report-date notice is unavailable
  before the cutoff time.
- Regulation: CSRC, SSE, SZSE, BSE, HKEX for cross-listed names, company
  announcements, exchange inquiry letters, shareholding changes, and
  refinancing/M&A filings.
- Policy and government: State Council, ministries, official press conferences,
  local government major project lists, policy documents, and budget notices.

## Financial Media And Data Wires

- Required Chinese authority checks: Cailian Press（财联社）, Caixin（财新）,
  Xinhua Finance, China Securities Journal, Shanghai Securities News,
  Securities Times, Securities Daily, Yicai, and 21st Century Business Herald.
- Required international authority checks: Reuters（路透社）, Bloomberg,
  Wall Street Journal, Financial Times, CNBC, Nikkei Asia, and official company
  event pages.
- Use media mainly for discovery, context, and confirmation. Prefer primary
  sources when a policy, filing, or regulator action is material.
- Treat Cailian Press（财联社）, Caixin（财新）, and Reuters（路透社） as
  high-priority confirmation or context sources for market-moving policy, macro,
  cross-border, geopolitical, commodity, and industry-supply-chain events. If
  access is unavailable, record the gap instead of replacing them with unsourced
  commentary.

## Cross-Market Channels

- Global equity leads: S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dow, China ADRs, Hang Seng Tech,
  Nikkei, Korea, Taiwan semiconductor chain.
- FX and rates: USD/CNH, CNY fixing, US Treasury yields, China government bond
  yields, repo rates, liquidity operations.
- Commodities: crude oil, copper, lithium carbonate, iron ore, coal, gold,
  agricultural futures, shipping rates.
- Themes: AI semiconductors, servers, optical modules, autonomous driving,
  humanoid robotics, innovative drugs, military, consumer, real estate,
  brokerage, state-owned enterprise reform, energy transition.

## Collection Discipline

- Daily scheduled runs must check the report-date PBOC open market operation
  notice before broad news search. Use it as liquidity context in `资金方向/热度`
  and as a macro candidate only when the net operation amount, rate, or maturity
  structure is market-moving versus recent expectations.
- Search both Chinese and English sources for global catalysts.
- Cross-check important market-moving items against at least one authority
  source from the required Chinese or international media list when primary
  sources are not enough.
- Add sector-specific keywords, not only broad A-share keywords.
- For each important event, keep one primary source and one confirming or
  context source when possible.
- Capture exact publication or announcement time when available.
- Reject items that cannot be tied to A-share sectors, companies, indices, or
  tradable instruments.
